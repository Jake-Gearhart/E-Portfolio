Jake Gearhart

Online Communities

Speech given 03/04/2021

We live in an unprecedented time. Not only have social media and internet forums brought us together more than ever, but at the same time, a global virus also seeks to tear us apart. Our sense of community and friendship is rapidly evolving and there is a unique opportunity to stay on top of and embrace it before things eventually return to whatever normal may be. Today, I hope to convince you to reach out with your passions, join and improve an online community, and make a lasting impact on the people you meet there. To do so, I'm going to share my experiences this past year, answer some questions you might have about online communities, and encourage you to follow in my footsteps.
